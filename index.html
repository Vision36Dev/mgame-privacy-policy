Privacy Policy for mGame

Last updated: April 2024

Welcome to mGame. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Info: Name, email, and profile details provided during registration.
  • Game Data: Points earned, levels reached (Devil 1, 2, etc.), and play time.
  • Financial Info: UPI IDs or Bank details provided solely for redeeming points into real money.
  • Device Info: Device model, OS version, and unique identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Maintain your account and rank on the Leaderboard.
  • Process your Redeem requests (Bank/UPI/Gift Cards).
  • Calculate Referral Commissions for your team.
  • Improve gameplay experience and difficulty levels.

3. Third-Party Services & Ads

mGame uses third-party advertising networks. These services may collect data to show personalized ads. Watching ads allows users to earn extra play time or double their points.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security to protect your points and personal info. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You can view your history, update personal info, or use the Delete Account option in the profile section at any time to remove your data from our system.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the "Contact Us" section in the app.
