At mGame ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application mGame (the "App").

⚠️ Important: By downloading, installing, or using the mGame App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies, please do not use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect the following information you voluntarily provide:

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we collect your username, email address, and phone number.
  • Profile Information: Any additional information you add to your profile, such as a profile picture.
  • Communications: Information you provide when contacting our support team, including messages and feedback.
  • User Content: Any content you create, share, or generate within the App.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and hardware specifications.
  • Usage Data: Game progress, levels completed, scores achieved, play time, and in-app interactions.
  • Log Data: Server logs, crash reports, and diagnostic information.
  • Advertising ID: Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID) used for advertising purposes.
  • IP Address: Your device's IP address when connecting to our services.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and user data management
  • Google Analytics: Usage analytics and crash reporting
  • Ad networks (Google AdMob, Unity Ads): Advertising information
  • Social media platforms (if you choose to share)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: To create and maintain your account, process game progress, and deliver in-app features.
  • Improve Experience: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and enhance App functionality.
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account.
  • Leaderboards: To display rankings and compare scores with other players.
  • Advertising: To deliver personalized and non-personalized advertisements.
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our App and measure ad performance.
  • Communications: To send you updates, notifications, and support responses.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and legal obligations.
💡 Note: We use your game data (points, scores, play time) to calculate your ranking on our global leaderboards. This data is essential for the competitive features of mGame.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:
